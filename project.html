I completed this project during my second year of university (Semester 2) as part of the course 'Topics in Computer Science'. It was my first real introduction to AI projects, and it played a major role in motivating me to pursue a major in Artificial Intelligence.
MOSS is an automated similarity checker that is used by universities for coding assignments. The aim of this project was to find solutions for improving MOSS as it cannot detect LLM-generated scripts. The project looked at what methods and what combination of methods could surpass MOSS for cheating manually as well as its ability to detect LLM-generated code.
